# рҹҡҖ NewsSphere AI

### AI-Powered Smart News Platform for Modern & Accessible News Consumption

NewsSphere AI is a next-generation news platform that combines **real-time global news**, **AI summarization**, **voice accessibility**, and an immersive **reels-style experience** into a single modern web application.

Designed with accessibility and engagement in mind, the platform helps users consume news faster through AI-generated summaries, audio narration, and simplified explanations for complex articles.
